About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Software Developer with a strong foundation in backend and full-stack development. My work focuses on building scalable, high-performance systems using modern technologies like Java, Scala, Python, JavaScript, React, Spring Boot, and microservices.

With solid knowledge of object-oriented programming (OOP) and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD), I take pride in writing clean, maintainable, and robust code that exceeds client and team expectations. I am well-versed in software development lifecycle (SDLC) principles and thrive in agile, fast-paced environments where adaptability, collaboration, and problem-solving are key.

My technical toolkit includes hands-on experience with:

  • Cloud Platforms: AWS, Google Cloud
  • Distributed Systems: Kafka, Kubernetes
  • Observability & Monitoring:Datadog, OpenTelemetry, Grafana

Before entering software development, I came from a background in health sciences, which sharpened my analytical thinking, attention to detail, and methodical approach to solving complex problems. These skills translate well into tech, helping me contribute effectively in both technical and cross-functional teams.

When I’m not coding, you’ll likely find me exploring new programming paradigms, reading tech books, or enjoying quality time with my family.
